3-day weekly plan

Day 1: Push + Core

  • Push-up progression
  • Dips (assisted if needed)
  • Planks

Day 2: Pull + Grip

  • Pull-up progression
  • Rows (rings/band)
  • Dead hangs

Day 3: Legs + Mobility

  • Split squats
  • Calf raises
  • Hip mobility
